Who We Are

Built for long-term
compounding

Asymmetrix, founded by entrepreneur-investor Greg Upham, is a family office based in Charlotte, NC focused on compounding capital over the long-term with an emphasis on alternative assets.

We define alternative assets broadly as almost anything that is not traditional public equities or fixed income. Our approach is patient, opportunistic, and conviction-driven.
