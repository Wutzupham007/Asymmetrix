About

Built on
conviction investing

Asymmetrix, founded by entrepreneur-investor Greg Upham, is a family office based in Charlotte, NC focused on compounding capital over the long-term with an emphasis on alternative assets.

We define alternative assets broadly as almost anything that is not traditional public equities or fixed income — giving us the flexibility to pursue the most compelling risk-adjusted opportunities wherever they arise.

Our approach is concentrated and long-horizon. We invest where we have genuine conviction, and we hold.
